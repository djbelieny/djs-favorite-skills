# Social Media Manager

This skill handles end-to-end social media management — from content strategy and creation to scheduling, publishing, and performance analysis across all major platforms.

## When to Use This Skill

- Creating social media posts (captions, hashtags, hooks)
- Planning content calendars (weekly, monthly)
- Scheduling posts across platforms
- Repurposing content across platforms (blog → social, video → clips, etc.)
- Analyzing engagement metrics and performance
- Building community engagement strategies
- Creating platform-specific content (Reels, Stories, Threads, Carousels)
- Hashtag research and optimization
- Trend identification and newsjacking
- Social media audits

## Platform Playbooks

### Instagram

**Content Types (by priority):**
1. **Reels** (highest reach) — 15-90 seconds, hook in first 3 seconds
2. **Carousels** (highest saves) — 5-10 slides, educational or storytelling
3. **Stories** (engagement) — polls, questions, behind-the-scenes
4. **Static posts** (brand building) — quotes, announcements, testimonials

**Caption Framework:**
```
[Hook — first line that stops the scroll]

[Value — the meat of your message, 2-4 short paragraphs]

[CTA — tell them what to do: save, share, comment, click link]

.
.
.
[Hashtags — 5-15 relevant, mix of sizes]
```

**Best Practices:**
- Post Reels 4-7x/week for maximum growth
- Use trending audio (check Reels tab for popular sounds)
- Carousel first slide = bold statement or question
- Reply to every comment within the first hour
- Use Stories daily to stay top of feed

**Optimal Post Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-2 PM, 7-9 PM (audience timezone)

### Facebook

**Content Types:**
1. **Video/Reels** (highest organic reach)
2. **Photo posts** with storytelling captions
3. **Link posts** (drive traffic)
4. **Live video** (highest real-time engagement)

**Best Practices:**
- Native video outperforms YouTube links 10:1
- Facebook Groups drive more engagement than Pages
- Ask questions to trigger comments (algorithm boost)
- Longer captions (1-3 paragraphs) perform well

**Optimal Post Times:** 9-11 AM, 1-3 PM (weekdays)

### X / Twitter

**Content Types:**
1. **Threads** (highest engagement + follows)
2. **Quote tweets** with hot takes
3. **Single tweets** with strong hooks
4. **Polls** (algorithm boost)

**Thread Framework:**
```
Tweet 1: [Bold claim or question — the hook]
Tweet 2-6: [Evidence, examples, or steps]
Tweet 7: [Summary + CTA to follow/retweet]
Last tweet: [Plug — your product, newsletter, or link]
```

**Best Practices:**
- Engage in replies for 15-30 min after posting
- Use 1-2 hashtags max (or none)
- Quote tweet > retweet (add your take)
- Pin your best-performing tweet or thread
- Post 3-5x/day minimum for growth

**Optimal Post Times:** 8-10 AM, 12-1 PM, 5-6 PM

### LinkedIn

**Content Types:**
1. **Text posts** with personal stories (highest organic reach)
2. **Document/carousel posts** (educational, high saves)
3. **Video** (native, under 2 min)
4. **Articles** (thought leadership, SEO value)

**Post Framework:**
```
[Hook — bold first line with line break]

[Story or insight — personal, vulnerable, or contrarian]

[Lesson or takeaway — actionable]

[CTA — agree? disagree? what's your experience?]

[3-5 hashtags]
```

**Best Practices:**
- First-person storytelling outperforms corporate speak
- "I" posts beat "we" posts by 3x
- Edit for mobile (short paragraphs, line breaks)
- Engage heavily in comments (first 90 min critical)
- Dwell time matters — write posts people pause on

**Optimal Post Times:** 7-9 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM (Tue-Thu best)

### TikTok

**Content Types:**
1. **Short-form video** (15-60 sec) — hook in first 1 second
2. **Stitches/Duets** (ride trending content)
3. **Photo carousels** (new format, growing fast)
4. **Live** (direct engagement, gifting)

**Video Framework:**
```
Second 0-1: [Pattern interrupt — visual hook]
Second 1-3: [State the promise — "Here's how to..."]
Body: [Deliver value fast — no fluff]
End: [CTA — follow for more, comment X, save this]
```

**Best Practices:**
- Vertical 9:16 only — fill the screen
- Use trending sounds (even at low volume under voiceover)
- Text overlays are critical (many watch without sound)
- Post 1-3x/day for algorithm favor
- Batch create — film 10 videos in one session

**Optimal Post Times:** 7-9 AM, 12-3 PM, 7-11 PM

## Content Calendar Planning

### Weekly Template
```
Monday:    Educational/value post (carousel or thread)
Tuesday:   Behind-the-scenes / personal story
Wednesday: Promotional (soft sell, case study, testimonial)
Thursday:  Engagement bait (poll, question, hot take)
Friday:    Fun/relatable content (meme, trend, humor)
Saturday:  Repurpose best performer from the week
Sunday:    Planning + community engagement (no new post)
```

### Content Pillars (customize per business)
1. **Educate** (how-tos, tips, tutorials) — 40%
2. **Inspire** (success stories, testimonials, behind-the-scenes) — 25%
3. **Entertain** (trends, memes, relatable content) — 20%
4. **Promote** (offers, launches, CTAs) — 15%

## Cross-Platform Repurposing

| Original Content | Instagram | Facebook | X | LinkedIn | TikTok |
|-----------------|-----------|----------|---|----------|--------|
| Blog post | Carousel summary | Link post + excerpt | Thread (key points) | Article or text post | Talking head summary |
| Video (long) | Reel (best 60s) | Native video | Clip + thread | Native video | Full or clips |
| Podcast | Audiogram Reel | Audiogram | Quote + link | Key takeaway post | Clip with captions |
| Customer review | Story + static post | Photo post | Quote tweet | Case study post | Reaction video |
| Event/launch | Countdown Stories | Event post | Announcement thread | Article | Hype video |

## Hashtag Strategy

**Instagram (15 hashtags):**
- 5 small (< 50k posts): niche, specific to your content
- 5 medium (50k-500k): broader niche terms
- 5 large (500k-5M): popular but relevant

**X (0-2 hashtags):** Only if trending or highly relevant

**LinkedIn (3-5 hashtags):** Industry-specific, mix branded + generic

**TikTok (3-5 hashtags):** 1 trending + 2-3 niche + 1 branded

## Analytics & Performance

### Metrics That Matter

| Metric | What It Means | Target |
|--------|---------------|--------|
| Reach | How many unique people saw your post | Growing month-over-month |
| Engagement Rate | (Likes+Comments+Saves+Shares) / Reach | > 3% Instagram, > 1% others |
| Saves | People bookmarking for later | High saves = valuable content |
| Shares | People sending to others | High shares = viral potential |
| Follower Growth Rate | New followers per week/month | > 1% per week |
| Click-Through Rate | Clicks to link / Impressions | > 1% |
| Best Performing Content | Top 3 posts by engagement | Replicate what works |

## Output Format

When creating a social media post, always provide:
1. **Platform(s)** targeted
2. **Post type** (reel, carousel, static, thread, etc.)
3. **Caption/copy** (platform-optimized)
4. **Hashtags** (if applicable)
5. **Visual direction** (what image/video should show)
6. **Best time to post** (based on platform + timezone)
7. **CTA** (what action the audience should take)

When planning a content calendar, provide:
1. Calendar view (day-by-day for the week/month)
2. Content pillar distribution
3. Platform assignment per post
4. Visual asset needs
5. Key dates/events to leverage
