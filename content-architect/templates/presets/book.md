# Book Structure Preset

## Terminology
- **Sections** = Parts (optional grouping of chapters)
- **Units** = Chapters
- **Opening** = Front Matter (preface, foreword, introduction)
- **Closing** = Back Matter (conclusion, about author, acknowledgments, bibliography)

## Typical Structure

### Front Matter
1. Half-title page (title only)
2. Title page (title, subtitle, author)
3. Copyright page
4. Dedication (optional)
5. Table of Contents
6. Preface/Foreword (optional)
7. Introduction (optional, if separate from Chapter 1)

### Body
- Organized into Parts (optional) and Chapters
- Each chapter starts on a new page
- Typical chapter length: 3,000-8,000 words
- Total: 40,000-80,000 words for non-fiction

### Back Matter
1. Conclusion (if separate from final chapter)
2. Epilogue (optional)
3. About the Author
4. Acknowledgments
5. Bibliography/References
6. Index

## Architecture Patterns

### Linear/Sequential
Best for: narrative non-fiction, memoirs, step-by-step guides
- Chapter 1 flows naturally into Chapter 2, etc.
- Strong chronological or logical progression

### Problem-Solution
Best for: self-help, business, transformation books
- Part I: Define the problem
- Part II: Explore causes and context
- Part III: Present the solution/method

### Journey-Based
Best for: spiritual, personal development, transformation
- Beginning: Current state, awakening
- Middle: Struggle, discovery, transformation
- End: New reality, integration

### Modular
Best for: reference books, toolkits, how-to guides
- Standalone chapters that can be read in any order
- Each chapter is a complete unit

### Framework-Based
Best for: methodology books, teaching systems
- Each chapter presents one principle/pillar
- Building blocks that construct the complete framework

## Chapter Template
1. **Opening hook** (story, question, or bold statement)
2. **Core teaching** (main argument with evidence)
3. **Practical application** (exercises, reflections, action steps)
4. **Bridge** (transition creating anticipation for next chapter)

## Word Count Guidelines
- Chapter: 3,000-8,000 words
- Preface: 1,000-2,000 words
- Introduction: 2,000-4,000 words
- Conclusion: 2,000-4,000 words
- Total manuscript: 40,000-80,000 words (non-fiction)
